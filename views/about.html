About ctrl-class

Our goal is to make your experience as a student as easy as it can be. We've made a tool for you to organize your classes, projects, and to-do's all in one place.

Tips

  • Click "Add New Course" on the courses page or "add course" from your course list on any page to add classes to your roster
  • From any course page, click on "Create a new project" to add a project for your class
  • From any project page, click on "add a new task" to add to-do's for the project you're working on
  • From any course or project page, see the tasks you have yet to accomplish
  • Groups can share projects and tasks so when someone finishes a task in a project, all group members can see what is done!
